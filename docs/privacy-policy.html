⏱️ Tickdrant – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

🔍 Overview

Tickdrant is a macOS task management application that helps you track countdown tasks and organize them by urgency and importance. Your privacy is important to us. This application is designed to operate entirely on your Mac and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server.

📵 Data We Do Not Collect

Tickdrant does not collect, request, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, postal address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Any information that could be used to identify you or your device

The app makes no network requests. Nothing ever leaves your device.

💾 Data Stored Locally on Your Mac

The following data is stored only on your Mac, inside the app's private App Sandbox container, and is never uploaded anywhere:

  • Your tasks – task names, due dates, recurrence settings, and completion state, saved as a JSON file inside the app's sandbox container.
  • Your preferences – display mode (Dock / menu bar / both), launch-at-login toggle, and notification permission state, saved via macOS UserDefaults.

You can export or import your task data at any time from Settings → Data → Export Tasks… / Import Tasks….

🔐 System Permissions

Tickdrant may ask you to grant the following macOS permissions. Each is optional and can be revoked at any time in System Settings:

  • Notifications – used only to remind you of upcoming tasks. Notifications are generated locally on your Mac; nothing is sent to any server.
  • Launch at Login – used only if you enable the toggle in Settings, so Tickdrant starts automatically when you log in.
  • User-selected files – Tickdrant reads or writes a file only when you explicitly pick one via the Export / Import dialog. It has no access to any other file on your Mac.

🚫 Third-Party Services

Tickdrant does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, crash-reporting, or cloud-sync services.

👶 Children's Privacy

Tickdrant is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

📝 Changes to This Policy

If our practices change, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
